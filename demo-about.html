About Us

Premier General Contractor and Pre-Engineered Metal Building Specialists

TOTAL CONSTRUCTION SERVICES, INC. is a premier, local general contractor located in Northern Virginia. We also specialize in design and construction of Pre-Engineered Metal Buildings and Pole Barns. As a family owned business in the Fredericksburg, VA area, we are proud to be an authorized dealer/builder for American Buildings Company and Ceco Building Systems, two of the leading metal building suppliers in the country.

Company Mission

  • To provide our clients with the highest level of quality construction products and services; safely, efficiently, timely and at a fair, competitive price.

  • To provide customer satisfaction in all areas including timeliness, attention to derail and service-minded attitudes, guaranteeing our company longevity through repeat and referral buiness

Background

Keith Machleit

As the Owner and President of Total Construction Services, Keith enjoys that he is able to participate in many areas of the business, including getting his hands dirty in the building process.

Born in Illinois, Keith grew up in the Fredericksburg area and entered the construction field as a teenager while still in high school. He enjoyed working with his hands and creating something from nothing. He learned early on that resourcefulness, dependability, teamwork, persistence, diligence and integrity are important core values. as an adult, he continued in the field of construction and in 1989 began working for Total Construction Services. By 2000, Keith knew he wanted to build something for his future and his family and started his own small construction company. In 2005 he was given the opportunity to purchase Total Construction Services, Inc. and return to the field he is passionate about. It felt like a great fit to purchase a family owned and operated business to join his own family business.

Keith is the father of three sons that he hopes will one day join the business and carry it into the future. In his free time, he enjoys the outdoors, boating and spending time with his family. His wife, Becky, serves as Office Manager at total Construction Services, Inc. She is the oil that keeps things running in the day-to-day business.
